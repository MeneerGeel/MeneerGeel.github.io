About Me

Heyhey, I'm Robert! Be advised, this is the first time I actually tried to do something with HTML so if anything looks broken or off, that's probably because it is. Anyway, I did my bachelor in Industrial Design Engineering, and am now continuing its master, in the Human Technology Relations track.

I think what I like most about IDE is the first part of any project; the part where you thorougly analyse a problem to (try to) find out what actually needs to be adressed and what people really need, and the part where you still have complete freedom to choose how you go about solving that problem. From what I've heard so far, this is where tinkering could be a good tool. Next to studying I like making music, surfing, and making/fixing stuff (see pictures)!
